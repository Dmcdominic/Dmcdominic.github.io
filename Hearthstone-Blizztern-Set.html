Hearthstone - Blizztern Set

Background: During summer 2019, I was an intern at Blizzard Entertainment on the initial design team for Hearthstone. I would love to show the content that I worked on, but since it won't be released until mid-2020, it's under strict NDA for now. Even for the internal intern expo at the end of the summer, I wasn't able to present my work, so instead, I created a custom card set featuring every Blizztern. In lieu of the real Hearthstone expansions that I helped to design, I hope that this demonstrates the initial design process.
