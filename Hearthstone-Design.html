Co-op adventure concept 1 - Mechazod Tavern Brawl style (with more minions)

Defeat the boss together, before he grows too powerful, and overwhelms you with his own side-swapping minions.

Co-op adventure concept 2 - Hero swapping

Your hero swaps into place at the start of your turn. Share your mana crystals and board, but not your hand, deck, or hero health. But heroes must be defeated in order to lose the match. Allows you to build your adventure decks in parallel and find unique cross-class synergies. Opens up totally new cards that interact with your teammate, such as passing cards.

Co-op adventure concept 3 - Boss as third, displaced hero (minions optional)

The boss hero takes up part of the board, so that each player is able to take their standard turn. Boss behavior may be simliar to Mechazod, gaining buffs or doing damage between turns.
