--- # Front Matter layout: game-page title: A Touching Conversation display_title: true description: "A conversation game about touch. After filling out a preliminary survey, players on either side of the wall place their hands into the box. They will then be prompted with charades style questions based on the comfort level inferred from the survey. Players must attempt to communicate complex ideas using only their hands." background: "Intro to Contextual Practice (CMU) - Apr. 2019
