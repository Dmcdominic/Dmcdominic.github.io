Description

Law is a card game made for a standard 52-card deck in which the rules change as you play. Each turn, take 3 cards from the Draw or Discard pile, then discard 1. When you get a set, straight, or flush, you get a new Law, and the rules have changed! Collect 4 Laws to win.

My Contributions

This was a solo project for an assignment titled "The Frontier of Freedom" which required us to design and develop any game at all. I was excited about the idea of a game for a 52-card deck which played differently every time due to a large number of possible interactions. I came up with the core concept quickly, then spent weeks of playtesting in order to refine the rules and make the Laws more fun.
